About Dictate

Dictate is an innovative video conference platform that provides extensive functionality, including but not limited to real-time conversation transcribing. One of Dictate's most saught after capabilities is its ability to dynamically and efficiently format displayed transcribed information as questions and answers.

Innovative Technology for Effective Communication


Dictate your interview

Allow for more flexibility during your interviews. With Dictate there is no need to manually keep track of what's discussed. Dictate automatically processes the conversation and organizes it into a single output, consisting of questions and answers.

Dictate your conference

Use Dictate for fluid communications during virtual meetings. With this technology you can focus on the topic(s) at hand, and allow Dictate to determine what is necessary for future reference.

Dictate your chat

Chats of all kinds require acute attention and documentation. No longer will you have to worry about formulating your conversation synopsis. Dictate will intelligently keep track of everything discussed.

Dictate your collaboration

Working together is the future of meaningul success. Through groups collaboration, Dictate will allow for deeper connection between participants, while organizing and visualizing all communicated data.
